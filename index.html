Working at Fresenius Medical Care

Creating a future worth living. For patients. Worldwide. Every day.

This is our vision that unites all of us at Fresenius Medical Care. Our vision, mission and core values provide a clear framework and guidance for the behavior and actions of our diverse workforce all around the world.

What we stand for

Empowering people. Advancing care. Inspiring with our purpose and values.
Everything we do here is about making people’s lives better, simpler and longer. Our teams work cross-functionally to reproduce the complex function of kidneys with treatments that meet high quality standards, improve clinical outcomes, and are sustainable and compliant. Internally, we promote diversity and inclusion as key elements of a positive, supportive work environment. Our values support our mission to develop high quality products and services, to have a positive impact on the health and wellbeing of patients, and to lead Fresenius Medical Care towards a successful, sustainable future.

Our Values

It’s not just about what we do, but how we do it. Our core values translate our vision into daily work and guide all our actions, decisions, and behaviors, while helping us lead Fresenius Medical Care into a successful future as we lead kidney care through exceptional patient care and innovation.

Our corporate culture

Mission

We provide the best possible care. Sustainably in diverse healthcare systems. For a growing number of patients around the world. Fresenius Medical Care achieves optimal sustainable clinical, quality and technological standards in patient care through our commitment to developing innovative products and therapies.

Integrity

As the world’s leading provider of dialysis products and services, Fresenius Medical Care is aware of its responsibility and has a global compliance program in place.

It aims to ensure adherence to legal requirements and our internal company guidelines.

Sustainability

Caring is at the heart of what we do. This extends to our concept of sustainability. Managing sustainability successfully means creating lasting value – economically, ecologically and socially.

We foster an environment that promotes the interests of our stakeholders – including our employees. For us, this also means that we continuously implement global sustainability standards in our operations around the world.

What we offer

At Fresenius Medical Care, we understand that we need to attract talent, but more importantly, we need to retain it. So we want to ensure that our employees are committed to our corporate targets and values and that they are motivated to contribute to our long-term success. To do so, we establish an ongoing dialogue with our employees to identify key areas with the aim of boosting commitment, increasing performance, and ultimately improving patients’ quality of life. We offer a series of perks to help them acquire new skills, advance in their field of expertise, and promote their wellbeing. From gaining professional value to the training and work-life balance benefits we offer, working at Fresenius Medical Care makes for a rewarding career.

Career with a purpose

We offer an opportunity to create and deliver treatments that save and change lives for the better. We’ll support your ongoing development. And you’ll be part of a dedicated team of people who inspire each other to create the best possible healthcare outcomes each and every day.

Work-life balance

We want to empower people to deliver better care. Therefore, we promote a better work-life balance through flexible working hours, part-time models, the possibility to work from home, and more.

Learning and development

We offer participation in programs at world-class business schools, leadership development, regular training for our nurses, health care professionals and manufacturing staff and digital access to high-quality educational content for all employees worldwide 24/7.

Stability

Developing innovative products and continuously improving our renal therapies made us the clear market leader in the production of hemodialysis machines, with sustainable, profitable growth. This position provides around 112,000 employees with the stability and security they need to help improve the lives of our patients.

Connected culture

Joining Fresenius Medical Care means becoming part of a global team that welcomes everyone. We embrace the different backgrounds, cultures, experiences, and opinions of our colleagues, and nurture an atmosphere in which people feel safe, connected, and valued.

Local benefits

Our employees enjoy both local and global opportunities for growth and personal fulfillment. We offer local benefits designed to suit the requirements of the respective country and place of work to create ideal conditions everywhere.
